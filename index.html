Chili Publish

Chili is a configurable UI for self-service branded document creation. I have experience using Chili Publish to create templates from input of the organization. Once created, users with a variety of skill sets – from beginners to seasoned graphics professionals – can edit these templates online. The edited templates are then delivered in the proper omnichannel format to the marketing automation program that served the organization. The advantage of Chili was that it allowed marketers with no graphic design skills to create complex, multi-page documents and it also allows for inline text editing.

Seismic Live Docs

Seismic allows markup on Microsoft word and Powerpoint documents in order to make them into editable documents in the Seismic Sales enablement platform. Seismic also has the ability to leverage data from Salesforce. With Seismic, sales and marketing are connected by an A.I.-powered platform. Sales reps are able to find and assemble the right, personalized content themselves without needing to pull in marketing to fulfill one-off requests. This means marketing stays in control of the messaging and sales can focus on selling, while every aspect of the content – from corporate branding to compliance.
